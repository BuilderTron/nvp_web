How we Capture your Special Event

Consultation & Flexibility

Our first step to any event is to establish a friendship. We want to get to know you, to know what you like and what your expectations are so that we can plan your event with utmost flexibilty.

Before the Event Photoshoot

Want a photo in the perfect place? We’ve got you covered! Natural setting photography, like a fountain in a park or field full of flowers, makes a dramatic statement to share with friends and family. Our adept team knows just what to do to pose you and your party in any outdoor setting.

THE MORNING OF YOUR EVENT

Our team of professionals will arrive in the morning to capture any transformation. From the glimpses of hair and makeup to organic and intimate moments of family and friends getting ready. Taking photos and video of the dress, shoes, and accessories as the big day begins.

Event Venue Photography

From the table tops to the centerpiece, every detail is a reflection of our clients vision and dream. Before you even arrive, we film and photograph every venue’s decor.

During Your Event

High Quality and Hard Work are just a few things you can come to expect as we work for you on your next event. During each event we make sure to capture all the presentations, dancing, food, laughter, and good times.

After Your Event

Our team will expertly edit, adjust, and compile the film and video from your special day into any package of your choosing.
